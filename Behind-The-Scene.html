BEHIND THE SCENES

"LAYOUT ARTISTS"

This website started as a blank white page with an ideas in our minds.

What followed was a journey of trial and error of HTML, CSS, and JAVASCRIPT codes, late-night debugging, and endless lines of errors that sometimes somehow worked and sometimes didn’t.

We experimented with animations, interactive designs, and randomized elements such as that polaroid pictures.

Learning how to turn these concepts into something more alive on the screen is very crucial, mind-boggling, and actually very fun. Every error in codes taught us patience and every breakthrough taught us persistence.

For us, building this website isn’t just about learning resilience, collaboration, and creativity. Just like our main objective for this publication, it tells that even in small ways, we can use our skills to speak against corruption, to shine the bright light on justice, and to remind people that truth has a voice.

- VOX VERITAS

"Editor in Chief"

We are an Editor in Chiefs and with that position we stood at the center of all the polishing thoughts and ideas. Every revision, every late-night editing and addition of new texts reminds us that leadership isn’t about control. It’s about clarity, patience, and purpose.

"CONTRIBUTORS"

We faced our blank screens and moments of doubt, yet each breakthrough reminded us why we began here. It wasn’t just about finishing our articles or meeting our subject deadlines; it was all about expressing truth, creating impact for people, and realizing that even small acts like this one can spark awareness. This publication became more than a school project as it became our statement. A statement that when used with purpose it can actually fight corruption, echo justice, and remind people that truth will always find its voice.
